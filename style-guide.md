### ðŸ’» Typography: Authority Meets Digitization

We need a font pairing that is **highly readable** for long-form analysis (SEO-driven content) but features a **modern, geometric flair** for headers and titles to emphasize the technology and future focus.

#### 1. Primary Font (The Brand Voice)
* **Font**: **Roboto** or **Montserrat** or similar.
* **Style**: Clean, highly readable, modern, versatile sans-serif.
* **Usage**: All long-form text, detailed analysis, bullet points, table content, and mobile text.
* **Reason**: **Readability is paramount** for your high-volume SEO content (1,000+ word articles). These fonts are clear at small sizes and project a professional, modern feel.

#### 1. Visual Texture: Light & Shadow
* **Emphasis on Light Flares**: Use subtle, dark, radial gradients in the background to mimic the light effect in your banner and profile picture. The `#0099FF` accent color should be used sparingly but strategically to create a sense of **depth and illumination** against the black canvas.
* **Borders & Separators**: Avoid harsh lines. Use **1px borders** in the `#0099FF` or `#A8C3E5` color with low opacity (e.g., 50%) to separate content sections, giving a "digitized terminal" look.
* **CTAs (Call-to-Action)**: Buttons should use the **Accent Glow** (`#0099FF`) as a background or a subtle border, and text should be bright white or a darker grey, reversing on hover for a noticeable "activation" state.
